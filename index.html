My research approaches problems in clinical medicine with a computational lens. My goal is to develop algorithms and datasets that can drive AI technologies to support medical decision making. I co-host The AI Health Podcast and co-edit the Doctor Penguin AI Health Newsletter. I instructed the Coursera course series on AI for Medicine, and founded the AI for Healthcare Bootcamp Program. Previously, I completed my PhD co-advised by Andrew Ng and Percy Liang at Stanford, where I also received both my Bachelors and Masters Degrees in Computer Science.
