---
title: Aftertaste
start: Jan 14, 2019
---
Oh sweet nectarine,
Twice tasted,
Linger on.

The tragedy of a memory,
Is in its peeling,
Layer by layer,
Until only the sprout remains.
Still, live on.

Let lips tell
Words mouths cannot.
Eyes show
Pulses of running thoughts.
Just another
Minute, Word, Taste,
let me lie,
That'll be enough.
